Create and sell your NFTs

Create your collections

To start selling first you will need to create a profile. After that go to your profile page. Click "Add Collection" and set up your collection details. Now you have an empty collection and it's time to go to the next step - Adding NFTs to it.

Add your NFTs

To add an NFT to a collection you have to click "Add NFT", then as you set up its details you will have a dropdown with your collection choices. A minimum of 4 NFTs are required in a collection in order for you to be able to sell it. Simple as that. You are done here so lets move to posting them live on the market.

List them for sale

After you are done with the 2 previous steps your will see that the "Post on Market" button is now active. Once you click and confirm you are done. You can monitor how the collection is doing on the market and how much are sold. After every purchase the crypto is send directly to your balance.
